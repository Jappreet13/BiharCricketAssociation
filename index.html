About B.C.A

Its our privilage to announce that on dated 18 Feb 2017 the brand new website of Bihar Cricket Association is launched to connect the cricket activities of Bihar to each and every stakeholders of Bihar Cricket Association.
This step is an approach to spread equality among each individual. We are trying to make cricket more than active and transparent ever in state. We have made all efforts to provide relevant information to the cricketing fraternity and fans, and are constantly endeavouring to keep our website updated.
Should you feel this website lacks certain information or may have suggestions to help us improve our website, please feel free to contact us - info@biharcricketassociation.com.
